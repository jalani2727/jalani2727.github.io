About Me

Less Words!

In 2017, I graduated from Emory University in Atlanta, GA as a student of the biology. Since 2019 I have played a role in the successful deployment, enhancement, and maintenance of SFRA eCommerce websites for a number of enterprise-level brands including Carter's, Johnston & Murphy, Fit2Run, ReserveBar and many more. For each of these projects I have implemented SalesForce Commerce Cloud development practices, utilized custom frameworks, built custom components and communicated directly with clients or teams of marketing professionals to identify and implement designs over the lifecycle of their website.

While I am most intimately familiar with eCommerce development architecture, my skills include JS/HTML/CSS, Vue.js, Bootstrap and SFRA. I am also well-versed in version management and project management tools such as JIRA, BitBucket and Confluence for documentation.

I seek to work alongside a talented group of like-minded developers to get work done together and learn from one-another.
